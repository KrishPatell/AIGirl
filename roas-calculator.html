FAQ

Everything you need to know about ROAS

1. What is ROAS?

ROAS stands for Return on Ad Spend—it’s how much you make for every $1 spent.

2. How to calculate ROAS?

Use the ROAS formula: Revenue ÷ Ad Spend. Our calculator does it instantly.

3. What’s a good ROAS?

Depends on your industry, but generally 3–4x is solid. Use break‑even ROAS to see where profit starts.

4. ROAS vs ROI—what’s the difference?

ROI includes all costs; ROAS focuses only on ad spend. Both matter, but ROAS reveals ad performance.

5. Can this help me increase ROAS?

Yes—by showing which campaigns to scale and which to cut, you’ll get more return for every dollar.
